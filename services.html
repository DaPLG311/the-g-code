Operator Field Notes

Figure out what you actually need.

Decide

Custom GPT, or just a simple page?

Rough rule: if you need to say something to the world, you need a page. If you need to do something repeatedly — answer the same questions, qualify leads, handle intake — you need a GPT or system. Many operators end up with both: a page that brings people in, and a GPT that handles them once they're there.

Not ready yet?

What if Same-Day MVP isn't the right step?

That's fine — pushing you into the wrong thing helps no one. Softer next steps:

  • A short consult to get clear on the move
  • A G Vault™ pack to do it yourself
  • The G Code Live™ room to learn first
  • A prep session to get your pieces ready
